## Usage notes

- **Badge with bg** — use on Carol's brand black canvas (`#201e1d`) where the bg blends naturally, or on any photo where you want a contained badge mark.
- **Transparent badge** — use on any background other than brand black. The double gold ring frames the typography on its own.
- **Clear space** — keep at least 10% of the badge diameter clear around the mark.
- **Minimum size** — do not display smaller than 64×64 px on screen.
